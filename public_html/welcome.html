Schedule.me

Schedule.me can help any business that employs part-time, shift work employees. We provide a seamless way to deliver schedules to employees, and make it easier for your employees to trade or get rid of shifts when necessary.

If you would like to discuss how Schedule.me can work with your business, please contact me at nrmitchi@gmail.com

Features:

Instant Publishing

Your employees will be notified as soon as schedules are published, and we able to access their schedules from any web-enabled device

Easy Upload

You can easily upload your schedule in whatever format your scheduler uses. If your format is not currently supported, we will work quickly to get it working for you

Easy Trading

Your employees can easily offer shifts to co-workers and pick up extra shifts when needed, with you having the final say on all changes.

Pricing

We make pricing simple, at just 50¢ per employee per month.
